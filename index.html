Help Center

How to Use

  • 1. Fill Header — Enter Date, Client, Project, Ref No., and Quotation Number at the top.
  • 2. Add Items — Click + Add Row or type number of rows and click Create Rows.
  • 3. Fill Each Row — Enter Item/Supplier name, upload image, fill Cost and PDM. Selling and Qty required for computation.
  • 4. Adjust Markup — Default is 40%. Change % per row if needed.
  • 5. Check Summary — Summary panel auto-updates as you type.
  • 6. Add Breakdowns — Optional Goods and PDM Breakdown sections.
  • 7. Sign — Fill signatory names at the bottom.
  • 8. Export — Click Export PDF to save or print.
  • 9. Multiple Quotes — Use tabs at top. Click ＋ New Quote. Double-click tab to rename.
  • 10. Auto-Save — Saves to the cloud automatically every 3 seconds.

Goods & PDM Breakdown

  • Click + Add Goods Breakdown or + Add PDM / Customized Breakdown
  • Each section starts with 1 row — click + Add Row to add more
  • You can add multiple sections — click button again for Goods 2, 3, etc.
  • Click ✕ Remove to delete a section
  • EWT fields are manual — enter the amount yourself

Image per Row

  • Click the image cell — focuses it (enables Ctrl+V paste)
  • Insert button — opens file picker to upload from your device
  • Ctrl+V — copy an image first, click image cell, then press Ctrl+V. Right-click paste not supported by browsers.
  • Right-click — shows menu: Upload Image, Remove Image
  • Backspace / Delete — focus the cell then press to remove image
  • Replace — Insert or paste a new image over existing one
  • Images auto-fit — portrait, landscape, or square — no distortion

Field Definitions

CostBase cost of the item from supplier.
PDMAdditional cost (printing, delivery, miscellaneous).
Total CostCost + PDM. Auto-computed.
MarkupTotal Cost × (1 + Markup%). Default 40%. Editable per row.
SellingYour selling price per unit. You enter this.
QtyNumber of units.
Total SellingSelling × Qty. Auto-computed.
Total Cost AmtTotal Cost × Qty. Auto-computed.
ProfitTotal Selling − Total Cost Amt. Auto-computed.
Profit %Profit ÷ Total Selling × 100. Auto-computed.
Gross MarginTotal Profit ÷ Total Selling × 100.
Net MarginGross Margin after 10% tax deduction.
EWTExpanded Withholding Tax. Optional, assumed by RTC.

Net Margin Guide

Minimum target: 20%

Net Margin = Gross Margin − 10% (tax). If gross margin is 30%, net margin is 20% — the minimum acceptable.

🔴 TOO LOW (below 20%) — selling price is too low relative to cost. You need to either raise the selling price or reduce the cost. You may also consult or seek approval from Sir Ram regarding this matter.

🟢 GOOD (20% and above) — acceptable margin.

How to fix a low margin:

  • Increase the Selling price per unit
  • Reduce Cost or PDM if possible
  • Check if Markup % is set correctly per item
  • Review Other Expenses % in the summary

Frequently Asked Questions

Why is my Net Margin red?
It's below 20%. Increase selling price or reduce costs.

How do I rename a tab?
Double-click the tab name and type the new name. Press Enter to confirm.

Is my data saved automatically?
Yes — every 3 seconds, saved automatically to the cloud.

Can others see my saved data?
Your quotations are stored in your company account. Only you and administrators can see them.

Keyboard shortcuts:
Enter — move to next row   Shift+Enter — new line in supplier field   Tab — next cell

Need help or suggestions?
Contact Executive Assistant Department or Isaac.

How do I install this as an app?

Option 1: Click the 📲 Install App button in the header if visible. Click Install when prompted.

Option 2: On Chrome, click the three dots (⋮) → "Cast, save, and share" → "Install page as app" → click Install.
